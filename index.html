Resume
I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at CMU and I work with Prof. Todd Mowry, Prof. Phil Gibbons and Prof. Tianqi Chen. My current research focus is on building better compilation and execution stacks for handling dynamism in deep learning models. In the past, I have worked on building compiler analysis techniques that understand and optimize programs written in general-purpose programming languages at semantically higher levels than is currently possible.
