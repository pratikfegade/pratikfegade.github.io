Resume
I am a fourth year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at CMU, advised by Prof. Todd Mowry and Prof. Phil Gibbons. My main research focus is to build compiler analysis techniques to understand and optimize programs at semantically higher levels than is possible now. I have also worked on resource bounds estimation and concurrent program verification during my internships.
